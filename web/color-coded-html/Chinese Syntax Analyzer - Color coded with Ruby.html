Full translation: Clinical and political reading The problem with modern genealogy is the short circuit between work and inheritance: parents do not pass on symbols, only products. The symbolic death of the parent (institutionalized by law) is necessary for the child's development—without it, the country becomes "Oedipal infantilized." Transgenderity is a rupture point in the system - the name of the father is reconfigured and enjoyment is shifted from the productive axis to the aesthetic and erotic axis.
